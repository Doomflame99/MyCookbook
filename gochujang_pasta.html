Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Mix hot spaghetti with pancetta, then add egg mixture.
  5. Serve with black pepper and extra cheese.
