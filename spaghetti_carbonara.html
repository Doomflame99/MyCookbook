Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Home
  • Recipes

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Egg yolks
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper

Instructions

Cook spaghetti. In a pan, cook pancetta and garlic. Mix egg yolks with Parmesan. Combine everything with the pasta, and top with black pepper. Serve immediately.
